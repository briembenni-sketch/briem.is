03 — Process

Four steps, no surprises.

A simple way of working that keeps things moving and keeps you in the loop.

  1. 01

    Talk

    We sit down — in person or on a call — and figure out what your company actually needs the website to do.

  2. 02

    Design

    I shape a direction that looks like you and nobody else, and we refine it together until it feels right.

  3. 03

    Build

    I hand-code the site to be fast, accessible and easy to run — tested on every screen size.

  4. 04

    Launch

    We go live, I set up hosting and analytics, and I stick around for the tweaks that come after.
